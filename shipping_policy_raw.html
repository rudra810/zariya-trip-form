SHIPPING POLICY

Last Updated: [20 Dec,2025]

We aim to deliver your order as swiftly and safely as possible.

1. Shipping Time

  • Orders are processed within 1–3 business days.

  • Delivery takes 3–6 business days depending on your location.

2. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping fees will be calculated at checkout.

3. Order Tracking

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS.

4. Delivery Attempts

If a delivery attempt fails due to incorrect address or unavailability, the courier may retry or return the order to us.

5. Delays

Unforeseen delays may occur due to weather, courier issues, or high volume. We appreciate your patience.
